Sanskrit Paradigms Memorizer

Instructions:

  • Select a paradigm from the options provided.
  • Choose the script (IAST or Devanagari) from the dropdown menu.
  • Use the "Next" button or the spacebar to move to the next item.
  • Use the "Previous" button or Shift + Space to go back to the previous item.
  • When you reach the last item, a “↺” symbol will appear. Click on it or press Enter to reset to the first item.
